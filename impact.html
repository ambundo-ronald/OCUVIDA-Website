Collection network

Impact begins before bottles reach the facility.

Glass recovery depends on people who collect from homes, businesses, informal recovery points, and aggregator networks. OCUVIDA's role is to connect that work to a more reliable receiving, sorting, and recycling pathway.

Impact story

Cleaner glass creates value at every stage.

When glass is collected separately and sorted well, it becomes easier to recycle, reuse, and upcycle. That improves the economics for aggregators and makes the downstream material more useful to partners.

BeforeBottles are scattered, mixed, heavy to transport, and often treated as waste.
DuringAggregators consolidate material and OCUVIDA sorts it into usable streams.
AfterPrepared glass can move toward recycling and reuse instead of disposal.
