What we do

Reliable glass bottle recovery from collection to recycling.

OCUVIDA gives used bottles a clear route out of dumping sites and back into productive use through focused collection, sorting, and recycling partnerships.

Operations

We handle glass as a dedicated material stream.

Glass needs careful collection and sorting. OCUVIDA keeps bottles separate from mixed waste, receives material at Kariobangi Light Industries, and prepares batches for the next stage of recycling or reuse.

Batch receivingAggregator loads are received in sacks, crates, or bulk collections.
Quality checksTeams remove unsuitable contaminants and separate reusable bottles.
Color separationClear, amber, and green glass are prepared as cleaner streams.

Aggregator collection

We coordinate pickups from aggregators who consolidate glass bottles from bars, restaurants, hotels, events, institutions, and estate collection points.

  • Pickup planning by county and volume.
  • Clear communication by phone or WhatsApp.
  • Support for recurring and one-off collections.
  • Receiving at Harmony Road, Kariobangi Light Industries.

Sorting and preparation

Bottles are weighed, checked, and sorted by color, quality, and reuse potential so each batch can move into the right recycling or reuse channel.

  • Green, clear, and amber stream separation.
  • Removal of unsuitable contaminants.
  • Preparation for recycling partners and upcycling uses.

Glass recycling partnerships

OCUVIDA works with businesses, county-level groups, and recycling partners to build cleaner glass recovery systems without mixing glass into general waste.

  • Business bottle recovery programs.
  • Community collection support.
  • Partner-ready glass material handling.

Community benefits

Glass recycling creates local work, supports dependants, reduces landfill pressure, and keeps bottles away from drains, roadsides, and informal dumpsites.

  • 32 staff supported.
  • More than 200 dependants reached.
  • Cleaner neighbourhoods and reduced waste volume.

Sustainability story

Every recovered bottle reduces waste and preserves material value.

Glass can be recycled repeatedly when it is collected and sorted well. OCUVIDA focuses on dependable logistics, clean material streams, and practical partnerships so aggregators can supply better glass and communities can see visible benefits.

Less dumpingFewer bottles entering drains, landfills, and open spaces.
More livelihoodsCollection work supports employees, families, and aggregator networks.
Better material flowSorted green, amber, and clear glass is easier to recycle, reuse, and upcycle.
